REVIEWER: Nick Feeney

NAME: Lotus Asia's Best

ADDRESS: 1819 Osos St

CITY: San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 2

SERVICE: 2

VENUE: 3

RATING: 2

WRITTEN REVIEW:

Lotus Asia's Best was quite a let down. I really was not impressed by the food. It was pretty cheap but it wasn't very good. The beer is pretty good there but they have a small selection. Overall the meal was just underwhelming shop some where else in my opinion.

The service at Lotus Asia's Best also wasn't great. I have to say that when we went there it was quite busy but the service took for every to set us. Then it took for ever for a waiter to come to our table. Finally, the food took a while to actually come. Maybe it was just cause it was busy but service seem poor when we went.

Lotus is an extremely standard restaurant. There is nothing really impressive about it except that it has Mongolian barbeque. If I ever go back I will have to try that out.

Overall i would not rate Lotus very well. I was not impressed when I went there. I will probably give it one more chance so I can try the only Mongolian barbecue in San Luis Obispo but I don't have very high hopes for it. I would say try another place if you have the choice.
