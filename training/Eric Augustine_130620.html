REVIEWER: Eriq Augustine

NAME: Margie's Diner

ADDRESS: 1575 Calle Joaquin

CITY: San Luis Obispo

FOOD: 4

SERVICE: 4

VENUE: 4

RATING: 4

WRITTEN REVIEW:

Margie's main theme is portions. It is like two meals for the price of 3/2. However, they don't sacrafice any quality. They food is great and in large portions. Also, they serve breakfast all day long, so that is a plus.

The service was fine. It was typical diner service. Nice people and quick serivce.

One of the best things about Margie's all of the little saying and easter eggs that they have around. They have a bunch of "No Whining" signs, you can pay like $1 for some guys phone number so you can talk to him, and other things like this.

Overall, it was a great brunch. Great food at a fun place. The food is a little expensive, but I believe that it is expected to have it over two meals. So, then it is pretty normal.
