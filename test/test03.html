WRITTEN REVIEW:

Vieni Vai offers authentic italian food at a somewhat reasonable price. The pizzas and calzones are handmade and cooked to perfection in a stone oven. Complementary bread is also served with a delicious olive and eggplant tapenade. Last but not least, Vieni Via offers a large assortment of deserts including a tiramisu that was featured on the Rachel Ray Show. Great quality food for those willing to spend an extra couple of dollars.

The service at Vieni Vai complements that of a italian restaurant; white table-cloths, prompt waiters, and refills on bread. The waiters are friendly and check on you periodically. The food is served in a timely manner and arrives hot from the oven. The waiters will happily provide their opinion on the foods and knowledgable on the ingredients of all the dishes.

Vieni Vai is located in downtown San Luis Obispo along the creek. The atmosphere is cozy and reminiscent of Italy, with authentic decor and mood lighting. During the day-time they also offer patio seating along the creek. This is definitely one of the nicer locations to dine at with its warm and relaxing, possibly romantic, atmosphere.

Overall, Vieni Vai provides excellent food and great service at a somewhat reasonable price. They have location great for any date or family gathering. So if you are looking for a place dine-in and enjoy authentic italian food, this place is for you.
